# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

Use welcoming and inclusive language.

Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

Accept constructive feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, and discriminatory language are not acceptable.

## Reporting

Please report conduct issues privately to the maintainers through the repository contact channel.
